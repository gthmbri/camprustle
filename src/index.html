RUSTIC CHARM. QUALITY FOOD. EXCEPTIONAL SERVICE.

Ticonderoga is an Iroquois word meaning “land between two lakes,” which is exactly where Camp Ticonderoga is situated. Located between Square Lake and Long Lake Roads, Camp Ti has been serving up delicious staples like pulled pork sandwiches, Certified Black Angus steaks and warm chocolate chip cookies since 1996.

Stop in to experience a warm, rustic atmosphere in our Adirondack-styled dining room, or dine al fresco overlooking the beautiful Sylvan Glen Golf Course from our outdoor terrace.
